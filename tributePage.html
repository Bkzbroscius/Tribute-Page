Some of Michael Jackson's Big Moments

Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson was born on August 29, 1958 and he died on June 25, 2009. He was known as the King of Pop and was one of the most influential figures in the music industry. He started his music career in 1964 as a member of the Jackson 5. As a solo artist, he released several albums including Thriller, Bad, and Dangerous. All three albums were a success and had several hit songs.

Thriller

Thriller was released on November 30, 1982. The album is a number one best seller and hits include "Thriller", "Baby Be Mine", and "The Lady in My Life". It won eight Grammy Awards in 1984 and its music videos were highly praised.

Bad

Bad was released on August 31, 1987 and is one of the 30 best-selling albums of all time. The album has several number one singles including "Bad" and "Smooth Criminal" and "Man in the Mirror."

Dangerous

Dangerous was released on November 26, 1991 as Michael Jackson's fourth album. Four of the singles made it to the Billboard Hot 100 including one number-one. Michael Jackson fully produced Dangerous. Hits include "Remember the Time", which received a Grammy Award and "Black or White."
